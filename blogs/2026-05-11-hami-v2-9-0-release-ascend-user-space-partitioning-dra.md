The HAMi community is proud to announce the official release of HAMi v2.9.0 . This represents a milestone version in terms of heterogeneous device virtualization depth, scheduler ecosystem expansion, and Kubernetes native standards alignment . v2.9.0 introduces the Ascend 910C HAMi-core mode, HAMi-DRA general availability, and Volcano vGPU upgrade to v0.19, along with systematic enhancements in observability, security, and stability.
